ABOUT

The Chitkara University Baddi Campus Sports Arena
is a modern facility designed to promote fitness,
teamwork and competitive spirit among students. Offering
a variety of indoor sports and outdoor sports,
the arena supports both recreational and professional-level
activities, contributing to the overall
physical and personal growth of students.
