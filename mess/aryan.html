Educational Blocks

Discover the state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated academic spaces designed to foster innovation, research, and holistic development.

Research Hub

Tagore Library

Named after the renowned poet and scholar Rabindranath Tagore, the library is designed to support academic and research needs by offering access to a wide array of resources.

  • Books and Journals: A vast collection of academic, reference, and general books spanning various disciplines, alongside national and international journals.
  • E-Resources: Access to digital platforms, e-books, research papers, and online databases perfectly tailored for modern students.
  • Study Spaces: Well-equipped reading rooms offering a quiet and comfortable environment for focused learning.
Innovation Center

Kalam Block

Named in honor of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President of India and a celebrated scientist. This block serves as a pivotal space for academic and co-curricular activities, embodying his visionary spirit.

  • Academic Classrooms: Equipped with modern teaching aids like smart boards, projectors, and comfortable seating arrangements.
  • Laboratories: Advanced labs for various engineering, sciences, and technology disciplines to provide hands-on experience.
  • Faculty Offices: Ensures accessibility for rigorous student mentoring and consultations.
Mathematics & Science

Ramanujan Block

Named after the legendary Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan, symbolizing academic excellence and intellectual curiosity. It houses facilities dedicated to advanced learning.

  • Specialized Departments: Serves as the hub for mathematics and computer science, fostering critical thinking.
  • Lecture Halls: State-of-the-art visual systems integrated into lecture halls to enhance experiential learning.
  • Research Labs: Computational studies and practical workstations designed exclusively for groundbreaking student projects.
Physics & Mechanics

CV Raman Block

Honoring Dr. C.V. Raman, the renowned physicist and Nobel Laureate, this block is an integral part of the campus promoting a deep research-oriented academic environment.

  • Engineering Focus: Houses intense focus groups related to physics and material sciences.
  • Research Centers: Advanced labs tailored to facilitate hands-on experiments and high-level innovation.
  • Smart Integration: Classrooms equipped with the latest teaching technology to bridge theoretical gaps.
Administration

Gandhi Block

The Gandhi Block acts as a core administrative and academic building. Found deeply integrated into the Chitkara University campus, it hosts vital functions required for the seamless operation of the institution.

  • Faculty Hub: Central location for numerous faculty lounges, student support services, and administrative staff.
  • Activity Hosting: Flexible spaces routinely utilized for seminars, cultural planning, and academic gatherings.
